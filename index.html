↓ Main Route – Land → Alpacas → Tekapo

10:40
Arrive Christchurch Airport (CHC) Christchurch
Land on SQ 297, clear immigration, pick up bags, grab the rental car.
~12:00
Sherlin Alpaca Farm Alpacas
Drive ~25 min to West Melton. Hand‑feed a big herd of alpacas, wander the paddocks, take photos and reset from the flight.
↗ View on Maps
13:15
Lunch stop – Geraldine On route
Head south via the inland route. Stop in Geraldine for lunch and a quick leg stretch; cute little town with cafés and a small bush walk if you want.
15:30
Arrive Lake Tekapo village Tekapo
Check into your Tekapo accommodation and take a breather – you’re now in the Mackenzie Basin with the turquoise lake right there.
16:00 – 17:30
Church of the Good Shepherd & lakeshore Tekapo
Walk to the stone church and along the rocky lakeshore. Take the postcard shots, breathe the cold air, and do an easy out‑and‑back along the lake edge.
17:30 – 18:45
Mt John Summit & Astro Café Tekapo
Drive or hike up to Mt John. Grab a coffee at Astro Café and soak the 360° views over Lake Tekapo and the Southern Alps, ideally around golden hour.
19:00
Dinner in Tekapo village Tekapo
Sit‑down dinner at Blue Lake Eatery & Bar (hearty Kiwi dishes & local beer) or Kohan (Japanese, lighter but still cosy), depending on mood.
21:00 – late
Stargazing & hot pools Dark Sky
Choose between Tekapo Star Gazing (guided night‑sky intro then floating in hot pools) or a Dark Sky Project observatory tour. Book ahead and hope for a clear, moon‑light night.
Christchurch Botanic Gardens & Hagley Park Christchurch
Huge green lung in the middle of the city. Great if you stay a night in CHC first or have a spare morning before flying out.
Rakaia Gorge lookout Inland route
Short viewpoint stop over the milky‑blue river gorge. Easy add‑on if the weather is clear on the way south.
Church of the Good Shepherd Tekapo
Stone church right on the lake edge. Classic Tekapo shot with mountains (and stars) behind it.
Lake Tekapo foreshore Tekapo
Rocky shoreline, big sky, and ridiculously blue water. Easy to access and you can walk as much or as little as you like.
Unknown Chapter Coffee Roasters Christchurch CBD
Strong coffee + brunch in the city. Easy first stop if you stay a night in CHC or swing back before flying home.
Hello Sunday Sydenham
Character brunch café in a former Sunday school; creative plates and good coffee.
The Greedy Cow Café Tekapo
Tekapo’s busiest café; big cabinet and cooked breakfasts with vego/vegan/GF options.
Astro Café Mt John
Coffee and cake with 360° views over Lake Tekapo and the Mackenzie Basin. Worth the drive on a clear day.
Grizzly Baked Goods Christchurch
Cult bakery for sourdough, cruffins and donuts. Good for breakfast on a CHC day or snacks for the drive.
Bellbird Baked Goods Christchurch
Artisan bakery with excellent sourdough and classic pastries. Another strong “load up before driving” option.
Little High Eatery Christchurch CBD
Trendy indoor food hall with burgers, dumplings, pizza etc. Good casual dinner if you spend a night in town.
Twenty Seven Steps New Regent St
Cozy upstairs bistro on a pastel tram street; seasonal NZ produce and a nice wine list.
Blue Lake Eatery & Bar Tekapo
Hearty Mount Cook salmon, lamb shanks and local beers. One of Tekapo’s most consistently good dinner spots.
Kohan Tekapo
Japanese restaurant on the lake with great bento and donburi; lighter but still feels like a “real” dinner.
Christchurch Gondola & Port Hills Christchurch
Cable car up to big views over the city, Lyttelton Harbour and the Alps. Easy win on a clear second day.
Riverside Market Christchurch CBD
Indoor food market with lots of small stalls and local products. Nice place to graze or pick up gifts.
International Antarctic Centre By CHC airport
Penguins, snowstorms, Hagglund rides. Very solid “do this near the airport” activity on arrival or departure day.
Hanmer Springs Thermal Pools Day trip
Alpine‑town hot pools and slides ~1.5–2 hours north of Christchurch. Great for a different kind of soak day.
Sherlin Alpaca Farm Tours West Melton
Family‑run farm ~25 minutes from CHC; hand‑feed friendly alpacas, chill farm vibe, easy photos.
Silverstream Alpaca Stud Near Kaiapoi
Another alpaca option north of Christchurch with structured tours and a small shop; good if you’re heading that way.
He Puna Taimoana New Brighton
Modern oceanfront hot pools by the New Brighton pier. Perfect for a sunset soak on your city day.
Tekapo Springs – Hot Pools Tekapo
Multiple hot pools themed on local lakes, plus spa and seasonal extras. Great late‑afternoon/early‑evening reset.
Tekapo Star Gazing (in the pools) Tekapo
Guided night‑sky intro followed by floating in hot pools under the Aoraki Mackenzie Dark Sky Reserve.
Dark Sky Project tours Tekapo
More telescope‑heavy observatory experiences – perfect if you want the full astronomy nerd treatment.
